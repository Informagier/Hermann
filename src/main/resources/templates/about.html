About us

We are Damian, Dominik and Jenny, three computer science students at HTW. Our project is a website for creating a better connection in the e-Sports community of Berlin.

Goals

  1. Improve communication and connections in-/between the different eSport clubs in cities
  2. Make eSport clubs more approachable
  3. Support (event) planning processes
